The Package Tracking App

Track and manage your online purchases with ease.


MailHaven’s package tracker for iOS is the online shopping and logistics assistant you’ve always wanted. The app automatically creates a shopping email for you to use, tracks your online purchases, consolidates retail promotions while eliminating spam, and alerts you whenever your package is delivered.



Automatic Package Tracking

When you shop online using MailHaven, tracking codes from your purchase confirmation emails are automatically synced to our system for a seamless purchase-to-delivery experience. Once synced, you’ll be able to view transaction details, delivery status, and more for each of your purchases.



Real-Time Delivery Alerts

Get notified when your package arrives. MailHaven alerts you whenever there’s a change to your order’s delivery status. Configure notifications to trigger whenever your package is in processing, on the truck, and when it’s delivered. When used jointly with the MailHaven smart mailbox, we’ll alert you the instant your package arrives.



Worry-Free Online Shopping

Say goodbye to junk folders and messy inboxes. MailHaven consolidates all incoming mail from the online retailers you shop at into one in-app location while ensuring your private information isn’t collected. Protect your data from retailers and access sales, promotion codes, and other coupons without being overwhelmed by retail spam.
