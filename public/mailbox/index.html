The Smart, Secure Mailbox

The future of package delivery is here.


MailHaven’s smart mailbox is a lockable, cell-enabled device that protects your packages the moment they’re delivered. Parcels are sheltered from the elements and thieves alike, safeguarding your packages until you arrive.



Unbeatable Package Protection

With the MailHaven smart mailbox, your packages aren’t just delivered - they’re secured. Your deliveries are protected from exposure and porch pirates through our weather-proof, locking mailbox. Unlock your mailbox with a security code or via our package tracking app for deeper integration.



No More Missed Deliveries

Never miss another package delivery again. Even when you’re away, your packages are safe and sound -- no more missed delivery notices and trips to the post office to pick up your long-awaited parcel. When used with the MailHaven iOS app, you’ll even receive real-time delivery alerts the moment your package arrives.



Sleek Design & Functionality

MailHaven’s IoT-enabled mailbox is modern, streamlined, and designed for the contemporary consumer. Crafted with residential neighborhoods in mind, the MailHaven mailbox is a stylish, secure curbside shelter for your incoming deliveries.

Better together.



The MailHaven mailbox works even better when used in conjunction with our package tracking app. Learn how MailHaven’s iOS app can protect your data, eliminate spam, and notify you about deliveries.
