How Communities are Leveraging Technology to Create a Better Quality of Life

Cities, subdivisions and communities are using technology to improve the standard of living and efficiency within their boundaries. Administrators believe that by adopting new technology, they can get a better return on their investment dollars, as opposed to investing in traditional alternatives. While most of these pilots or partnerships are new, we are yet to see comprehensive studies detailing the ROI on these initiatives. However, residents and studies indicate that people are willing to pay more for homes with smart technology, which can contribute to the overall increase in median home values.
